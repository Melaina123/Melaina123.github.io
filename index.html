What Problems Are We Facing?


An Alien Concept

Most people will go their entire life not knowing they can donate bone marrow or stem cells.This is due to the fact that this topic is not talked about and many donors do not want to be in the spotlight.


Keeping Volunteers Engaged

Some volunteers do not know the full process of doing a transplant, and therefore are not in the state of mind to make a conscious decision to donate. There are also false notions about donations that frighten some volunteers.


Lack of Diversity

The white population has a 90% chance of finding a match for donation. When it comes the Asian and Latino communities this number drops to 45% . As we look at other minorities it is down to as low as only 20%. Not only do we need more donations, we need more diverse donations.







What Is Our Solution?

Building A Community On The Basis Of Knowledge


Youth will receive frequent updates and information about what is happening in the medicinal world and
opportunities to participate near and far from a bot that connects to an API

Donors can chat with recipients or other donors in a safe online community

Building this foundation with the youth helps with trust and spreading knowledge.
